Springsong

Spring has sprung; a song has been sung – it’s time to get winning with Springsong! Reveal colourful birds chirping away in the birdhouses and discover what prizes they have nested away. Match 3 birds in a row to win! If you reveal a golden egg, you win that row’s prize instantly!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Press each Birdhouse symbol to reveal a Bird or Golden Egg symbol.

Press each Prize box to reveal the row’s prize value.

Find 3 identical Bird symbols in any row to win the row’s prize value.

Find a Golden Egg symbol to win the row’s prize value instantly!

When all the Birdhouses and the Prize boxes are revealed, the game ends. A closing message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. The base game results are shown instantly and any bonuses are played through without player interaction.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
